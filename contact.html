    FAQ

    Common Questions

    What is your appointment cancellation policy?

    We request that you reschedule or cancel your reservation at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled time may incur a fee of 50% of the service price.

    Can I register duplicate emails or passwords?

    Yes. To facilitate immediate testing and demo flows, our local authentication allows duplicate emails and passwords of any length. Your account data is securely stored inside your browser session storage.

    Are your skincare products organic and chemical-free?

    Absolutely. We utilize biodynamic active botanical products containing no silicones, formaldehydes, or parabens. We prioritize natural active ingredients that restore and feed the dermis.

    How does your membership system work?

    When you complete a booking in our Client Space, you earn loyalty points. Reaching 500 points elevates you to Gold status, and 1000 points to Platinum status, unlocking exclusive discounts and perks.
