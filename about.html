    About Us
    The Journey

    A Heritage of Organic Wellness

    “We set out to create more than just a salon; we crafted a holistic wellness ecosystem.”

    Founded by styling director Marcus Dupont and skincare specialist Elena Rostova, Stackly was born from a desire to harmonize advanced cosmetic techniques with restorative organic treatments. We observed how traditional beauty services prioritized quick results at the cost of long-term skin and hair health.

    By handpicking raw active botanicals, employing highly certified massage clinicians, and designing a quiet, sound-insulated resort aesthetic, we provide a true oasis. At Stackly, every guest receives a customized, detailed skin or hair audit to ensure absolute accuracy and safety.

    Milestones & History

    2014

    The Founding

    Stackly opens its doors in Salem with 4 treatment rooms and 5 master practitioners, pioneering holistic face mapping.

    2017

    Elite Hair Expansion

    We launch the Couture Hair Color Wing, introducing ammonia-free French Balayage treatments to southern India.

    Renovated the entire space to feature soundproof walls, hot volcanic stones, and active aromatherapy filtration.

    Stackly Academy Launch

    Established our training school to certify regional beauty practitioners in organic product chemistry.
